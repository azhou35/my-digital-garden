    # What product would you build

    # 1. Internal Data Platform

    • Purpose: Centralize massive datasets (market, alternative data, research data) so quants and data scientists can access, query, and analyze them efficiently.

    • Features:

      • Searchable datasets with metadata and quality indicators

      • Versioning and lineage tracking

      • Integration with analysis pipelines (Python, R, SQL)

      • Access controls and audit logs

    • PM Challenges: Prioritizing features vs. ease of use, scaling infrastructure, ensuring data quality.

    # 3. Research Collaboration Platform

    • Purpose: Enable researchers and quants to share models, experiments, and results efficiently.

    • Features:

      • Model versioning and reproducibility tracking

      • Notebook sharing and commenting

      • Automated experiment logging

      • Metrics tracking (accuracy, backtest performance, deployment readiness)

    • PM Challenges: Ensuring adoption, motivating usage, integrating with existing workflows.
