    # Choose-Your-Own-Adventure

    extend it into a short story narrative that can be playable as a choose your own adventure.

    open source tools: Twine / An open-source tool for telling interactive, nonlinear stories

    Story Framework: Opening: You wake up in your apartment after the procedure. Your friend is there to help you through the day, trying to carefully navigate what you now don’t remember.

    Core Journey: As you move through familiar spaces, you encounter things that should trigger memories but don’t. However, you begin experiencing “emotional echoes” - feelings without context.

    Key Choice Paths:

    1. Acceptance Path: Embracing your new beginning, actively avoiding triggers
    2. Investigation Path: Becoming curious about the gaps, seeking to understand what you’ve lost
    3. Reconnection Path: Accidentally encountering your ex and deciding how to proceed
