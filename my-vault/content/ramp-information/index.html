    # Spend Management Product Operations Summary ## Organizational Structure

    • Organized into pods (teams) that break down components into manageable pieces
    • Key pods include: Accounting, Bill Pay, Procurement, Card Platform, Integration, Fintech Platform
    • Travel is the newest product area, focusing on partnerships with other companies and finding product-market fit

    # Role Responsibilities

    • Work closely with Product Managers who set strategic decisions

    • Support product launches by managing:

    • Alpha testing - Beta testing (5K users) - General availability release

    • Document product stories and experiences

    • Collaborate with PM counterparts to measure qualitative metrics

    • Form and execute on roadmaps

    • Handle escalations in partnership with PM and tech leads

    • Create enablement processes and documentation

    • Coordinate with Product Marketing Managers for major features

    # Work Approach

    • Take a high-level strategic view while also going deep on specific areas
    • Serve as the “engine of release”
    • Provide high-level oversight while executing on the ground
    • Support PMs with unblocking development issues

    # Hiring Profile

    • Often recruit from strategy and operations backgrounds
    • Candidates from business schools with tech company experience
    • Value analytical ability, metric orientation, and cross-functional experience
    • Look for “slope” (growth potential) rather than just experience
    • Seek hard-working, ambitious candidates not afraid to “grind”
    • Position as potential career path into Product Management

    # Team Structure

    • Growing team of 12-15 people
    • Recently hired a manager
    • Team members typically in early to late 20s
    • Managers around age 34
    • Company (Ramp) described as “smallest big company”

    # Career Growth

    • Define your path by strengths: product vs. operations vs. research
    • Focus on building something meaningful
