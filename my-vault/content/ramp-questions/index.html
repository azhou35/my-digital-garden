    # Tell me about a time you automated a process

    • Sitch: its so easy for cloud costs to get out of control, and our team is no exception. we were wasting multiple k’s of $s bc people would provision VMs for demos and literally leave them running - and neve close them
    • Task: Knew something had to change, and the devs were just chasing down people one-by-one - deleting resources by clicking on each one and pressing the delete button - but with 4k resources I knew we needed a better solution
    • It’s not fun or sexy being the cloud resource police. But I spearheaded and enforced up a process of regular maintenance and clean up. It’s a simple solution, really, but I set up rules on how people should tag their resource if they wanted to keep it for longer than a month. And I wrote up a script that would automatically search for these untagged resources, and every month I would run it and it would check who hasnt tagged their resource, send out a warning email to their outlook, and if after a week they havent deleted it, it would automatically delete it.
    • Result is that we cleaned up over 3k+ subs, and I saved hours of headache for myself and for the devs.
