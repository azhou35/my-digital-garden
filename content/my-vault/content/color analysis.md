---
title: "color analysis"
---

season:
- cool/true summer
	- flattering colors:
		- neutrals like soft navy, grey, cool taupe, lavendar, dusty rose, cool rose pink, powder blue, plum, soft teal, perwinkle
	- avoid tomato red, orange, anything too bright or saturated
	- fabrics in matte/soft finishes (line brushed cotton jersey)
	- opt for monchromtic or analgous outfits
