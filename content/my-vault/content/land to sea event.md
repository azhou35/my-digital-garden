Long version:
Calling all sentimental folks, hobbyist journalists, and photographers - come to Land to Sea for Scrap & Yap, a cozy end-of-year junk journaling night.

Let’s slow down and make something with our hands. Bring your favorite photos and bits of 2025 (receipts, postcards, stickers, metro stubs), and we’ll help you turn them into a keepsake you’ll want to hold onto.

We’ll provide scissors, tape, markers, paper, magazines, and small printers with photo paper for on-site printing (each guest can upload up to **5 photos** for us to print beforehand). Come reflect on your year, meet other creatives, and leave with a tiny time capsule of your 2025.

Tickets are **$15** and cover all supplies.  
Drinks + café offerings available for purchase through Land to Sea.
