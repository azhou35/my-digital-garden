2. **Demographics and Signals**: Young office workers dominate plaza use, with a higher presence of women indicating a plaza's sociability and appeal.
    
3. **Human Behavior in Space**:
    
    - People gravitate toward crowds and edges (e.g., pillars), avoiding open central areas.
    - Social interactions cluster near traffic lines, not isolated zones.
