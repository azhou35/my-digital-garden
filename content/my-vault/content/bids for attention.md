title: "bids for attention"
---

A "bid" is any attempt from one partner to connect with the other, ranging from small gestures (showing them something interesting, asking a casual question) to more obvious requests for attention or support. The researchers identified three ways people typically respond to these bids:
1. turn towards and ack bid
2. turn away and ignore or miss bid
3. turn against by responding negatively bid
